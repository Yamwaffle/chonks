About

$CHONKS is the first Clan-2-Earn token in crypto space - where holders earn rewards by playing games together and against each other.

We introduce a new earning model that goes hand in hand with the Play2Earn hype, where players earn crypto by playing games that they already love. This is different from Play2Earn where you are limited to what usually is a mediocre game.
In Clan-2-Earn our clan of holders decides which existing games they would like to play and the rewards for playing or winning in those games will be funded by Chonk’s clan wallet. Can you imagine earning BNB by betraying your everyone in Among Us? Or perhaps you use the Egyptian God Cards to easily claim your prize in a YuGiOh Duel Masters tournament?
But building a thriving, mature gaming community is only the minimal potential of Clan2Earn.

As a real clan ought to do, we will CHALLENGE other token communities to heated wagers in competitive games, WIN, and IMMORTALIZE our name in crypto as the community that revolutionized the space and added a whole other level of fun to it. Let's all work together and share in the SPOILS OF WAR
